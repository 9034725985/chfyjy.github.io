Wayland Protocol

Wayland is a protocol for a compositor to talk to its clients.

Used in the Qt Wayland Compositor, and the Qt Wayland platform plugin.

The sources can be found in tqtc-qt5/qtwayland/src/3rdparty/protocol/wayland.xml.
